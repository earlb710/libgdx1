Background: Dark blue-gray (RGB: 0.1, 0.1, 0.15)
Title Color: Gold (Color.GOLD)
Subtitle Color: Light Gray (Color.LIGHT_GRAY)
Button Color: Dark gray (0.3, 0.3, 0.4)
Button Hover: Lighter gray (0.4, 0.4, 0.5)
Button Border: White
Button Text: White

Interactions:
- Mouse hover over buttons: Changes button color to lighter shade
- Click on PLAY: Transitions to MainScreen (game starts)
- Click on QUIT: Exits the application
- Window resize: Buttons reposition to stay centered

Screen Flow:
1. New User: LoginScreen → SplashScreen → (PLAY) → MainScreen
2. Returning User: SplashScreen → (PLAY) → MainScreen
